RF2 Classification --> Training without features of activation and terminal loops.

1. RF2-PhaseI-seed{*} files contain the feature scoring results from PhaseI, RF-based pre-screening classification.

2. RF2-PhaseII-seed{*} files contain the feature scoring results from PhaseII, RF-based classification. 

3. The features pre-screened from each iteration of PHASE I (RF2-PhaseI-seed{*} files) that contribute to 99% total MDI importance of classification are listed and scored in PHASE II RF-based Classification (RF2-PhaseII-seed{*} files). 

4. RF2PhaseIICompositeFeatureScore.csv lists the top 30 composite scorers obtained from five rounds of PhaseII RF classifications.
