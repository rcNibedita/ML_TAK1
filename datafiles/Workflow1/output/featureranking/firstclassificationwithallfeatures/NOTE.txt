RF1 Classification --> Training with all features of kinase domain.

1. RF1-PhaseI-seed{*} files contain the feature scoring results from PhaseI, RF-based pre-screening classification.

2. RF1-PhaseII-seed{*} files contain the feature scoring results from PhaseII, RF-based classification. 

3. The features pre-screened from each iteration of PHASE I (RF1-PhaseI-seed{*} files) that contribute to 99% total MDI importance of classification are listed and scored in PHASE II RF-based Classification (RF1-PhaseII-seed{*} files).

4. RF1PhaseIICompositeFeatureScore.csv lists the top 30 composite scorers obtained from five rounds of PhaseII RF classifications.
